Disease Prediction


HealthCheck is a platform designed to help individuals predict and understand their risk for diseases such as heart disease and diabetes. Our system utilizes advanced algorithms to analyze health parameters and provide insights into potential health risks.

Heart Disease Prediction

Our heart disease prediction model assesses factors such as blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and lifestyle habits to predict potential heart-related risks.

Kidney Disease Prediction
